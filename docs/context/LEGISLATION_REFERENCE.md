# Legislation & Standards Reference

## Primary Legislation - Workplace Safety

### 1. D.Lgs. 81/2008 - Testo Unico Sicurezza
**Title**: Testo Unico sulla Salute e Sicurezza nei Luoghi di Lavoro
**Published**: G.U. n. 101, 30/04/2008 | **Status**: In force (modified by D.L. 146/2021)
**Affects**: ALL documents

Core law for all workplace safety documentation. Key articles:
- Art. 17: DVR obligation (non-delegable)
- Art. 28: Risk assessment scope (includes stress and pregnant workers)
- Art. 29: Assessment methods
- Art. 26: DUVRI for contracts
- Art. 37: Training
- Artt. 43-46: Emergencies, fire
- Titolo III: Equipment and PPE
- Titolo IV: Construction sites, POS
- Titolo VI, Artt. 167-171: MMC
- Titolo VII, Artt. 172-179: VDT
- Titolo VIII: Physical agents (microclimate, noise, vibration)
- Titolo IX: Chemical, carcinogenic, asbestos
- Titolo X, Artt. 266-286: Biological risk
- Allegato XXXIII: ISO standards for MMC
- Allegato XXXIV: VDT requirements
- Allegato IV: Workplace requirements
- Allegato XV: POS contents
- Allegati XLIV-XLVI: Biological risk

**URL**: https://www.normattiva.it/uri-res/N2Ls?urn:nir:stato:decreto.legislativo:2008-04-09;81

### 2. D.Lgs. 106/2009 - Correttivo TU
**Title**: Disposizioni integrative e correttive del D.Lgs. 81/2008
**Status**: In force
**Affects**: DVR, DUVRI, all attachments

Corrective decree modifying 100+ articles. Key changes:
- Art. 28: Certain date for DVR, stress assessment
- Art. 29: Standardized procedures for SMEs
- Art. 26: 5 man-day threshold for DUVRI
- Recalibrated sanctions

**URL**: https://www.normattiva.it/uri-res/N2Ls?urn:nir:stato:decreto.legislativo:2009-08-03;106

## Technical Standards (UNI/ISO)

### 15. UNI EN ISO 7730:2006 - PMV/PPD Thermal Comfort
**Affects**: Allegato Microclima (moderate environments)
- PMV equation: f(Ta, Tr, Va, Ur, M, Icl)
- PPD = f(PMV)
- Comfort categories: A(PPD<6%), B(<10%), C(<15%)
- Local discomfort: air drafts, radiant asymmetry

### 16. UNI EN ISO 7933:2005 - PHS Severe Heat
**Affects**: Allegato Microclima Caldo Severo
- Thermal balance: M-W = Cres+Eres+K+C+R+E+S
- Dlim calculation (max exposure time)
- Criteria: rectal temperature, dehydration

### 17. UNI EN ISO 11228-1:2022 - NIOSH Lifting
**Affects**: Allegato MMC
- RWL = LC x HM x VM x DM x AM x FM x CM
- LI (Lifting Index): LI > 1 = risk

### 18. UNI EN ISO 14121-1 - Risk Assessment Methodology
**Affects**: DVR (methodology section)

### 19. UNI EN ISO 11228-2 - Pushing and Pulling
**Affects**: MMC (supplementary)

### 20. UNI EN ISO 11228-3 - Repetitive Movements (OCRA)
**Affects**: MMC (supplementary, not in current scope)
